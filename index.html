Joydepur,Railway junction

Alhamdulillah, the second project ended very well. Considering the current dengue situation in the country, this small effort of ours for all the people who sleep on the streets to make them smile. To the best of our ability, we have given mosquito nets to 40 people so that they can escape from mosquito bites and sleep peacefully at the end of the day. Thank you to all the members of Student Club of Gazipur and Interact Club of Rani Bilasmani Government Boys High School. Without them, the work might not have been done so beautifully. "In the interest of humanity, we will stand by the helpless."
